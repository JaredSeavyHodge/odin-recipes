SMOKED PORK TENDERLOIN

Give your pork a sweet and smoky kick.

These tenderloins are marinated in a sweet honey and thyme mixture, then smoked to perfection over aromatic applewood pellets.

Ingredients

  • 2 pork tenderloins
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup thyme
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Place pork tenderloins in a large ziplock bag and pour marinade over the pork tenderloins.
  3. Seal the bag and shake to coat the pork tenderloins.
  4. Place pork tenderloins on the grill and smoke for 1 hour.
  5. Remove pork tenderloins from the grill and serve.
