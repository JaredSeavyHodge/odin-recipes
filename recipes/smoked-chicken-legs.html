Smoked Chicken Legs

These smoked chicken legs are a great way to get your protein in for the day.

They are easy to make and are a great addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken legs
  • 1/2 cup ancho chili powder
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup paprika
  • 1/2 cup garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup onion powder
  • 1/2 cup black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Place chicken legs in a large ziplock bag and pour spice mixture over the chicken legs.
  3. Seal the bag and shake to coat the chicken legs.
  4. Place chicken legs on the grill and smoke for 1 hour.
  5. Remove chicken legs from the grill and serve.
