3-2-1 BBQ Baby Back Ribs

Our famously easy 3-2-1 ribs recipe will make your rib game the envy of the neighborhood.

This super simple recipe takes all the confusion out of making ribs without sacrificing any flavor. Start by smoking your ribs for 3 hours, then cook inside foil for 2 hours and finish by removing from foil and brushing on sauce for up to 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of baby back ribs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup paprika
  • 1/2 cup garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup onion powder
  • 1/2 cup black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Place chicken legs in a large ziplock bag and pour spice mixture over the chicken legs.
  3. Seal the bag and shake to coat the chicken legs.
  4. Place chicken legs on the grill and smoke for 1 hour.
  5. Remove chicken legs from the grill and serve.
